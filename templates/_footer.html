Privacy Policy

Data Collection
We collect only the URL/domain you submit for scanning and your account details (username, email, hashed password) if you register. No payment information is collected.

Scan Data
Scan results are stored in a local SQLite database tied to your account. They are not shared with or sold to any third party.

Report Storage
Generated reports (JSON, PDF, Excel) are stored locally in the /reports directory of the installation. You can delete these from your dashboard at any time.

No External Tracking
We do not use cookies for tracking, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. Session cookies are used solely for authentication.

Password Security
All passwords are hashed using Werkzeug's PBKDF2-SHA256 algorithm. Plaintext passwords are never stored.

Data Deletion
You may delete your scan history at any time from the dashboard. Account deletion removes all associated data.
